# Use Case – BlueContoso Trading Ltd
## Designing the Financial Foundation

---

## Company Overview
- Name: BlueContoso Trading Ltd
- Industry: Wholesale electronics
- Location: Malta
- ERP Status: Organization structure completed (Week 1)

---

## Business Situation

BlueContoso has now completed its **organizational setup**.

The next step is to define how the company will:
- Record financial transactions
- Track profit and loss
- Produce financial reports

Currently:
- Accounting is done in spreadsheets
- No standardized Chart of Accounts exists
- Limited ability to analyze performance

---

## Business Requirements

### Financial Reporting
- Monthly financial statements
- Profitability by department
- Cost tracking by business function

### Structure Requirements
- Simple Chart of Accounts (initial phase)
- Clear distinction between:
  - Revenue
  - Expenses
  - Assets
  - Liabilities

### Analysis Requirements
- Track results by:
  - Department
  - Cost center

---

## Currency Requirements
- Base currency: EUR
- Transactions may occur in foreign currencies (future)

---

## Key Business Questions

1. How detailed should the Chart of Accounts be?
2. Should reporting rely more on accounts or dimensions?
3. How should departments be reflected in reporting?
4. What happens if the COA is poorly designed?

---

## Expected Outcome

A financial structure that:
- Supports accurate accounting
- Enables management reporting
- Is flexible for future growth
