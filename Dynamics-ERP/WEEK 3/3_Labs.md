# Lab 03 – Vendor & Product Setup

## 🎯 Objective

Configure the **master data required for procurement** in D365FO.

---

## 📍 Scenario

You are preparing BlueContoso for procurement operations.

---

## ✅ Prerequisites

- Week 01 completed (Legal entity)
- Week 02 completed (GL & COA)

---

## 🔧 Task 1 – Create Vendors

1. Create at least 2 vendors:
   - Local supplier
   - International supplier
2. Define:
   - Name
   - Currency
   - Payment terms

---

## 📦 Task 2 – Create Products

1. Define products:
   - Laptop
   - Office chair
   - Accessories
2. Set product type:
   - Item or Service

---

## 🔁 Task 3 – Release Products

1. Release products to legal entity
2. Verify availability in company

---

## 🏷️ Task 4 – Assign Categories

1. Create procurement categories
2. Assign products to categories

---

## ✅ Validation Checklist

- Vendors created
- Products created
- Products released
- Categories assigned

---

## 🤔 Reflection Questions

- What happens if products are not released?
- Why is vendor data critical?
- How would duplicate vendors affect procurement?
